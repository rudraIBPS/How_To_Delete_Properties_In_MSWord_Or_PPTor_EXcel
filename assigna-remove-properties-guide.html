Remove File Properties
Before Uploading Your Assignment

Follow the steps below carefully to strip personal metadata from your file before submission. This ensures your identity and device information are not embedded in the document you upload.

⚠️

Important: Please complete all steps on your PC or Laptop (Windows OS). This process removes hidden metadata — such as author name, device details, and edit history — from your file before it is submitted.

Step-by-Step Instructions
1
💾
Step 1
Download the File to Your PC / Laptop
Locate the assignment file and save it locally to your computer. Make sure you know the folder where it is saved — you will need to find it in the next step.
2
🖱️
Step 2
Right-Click on the Downloaded File
Navigate to the downloaded file in File Explorer. Right-click on it to open the context menu. A list of options will appear on your screen.
3
📋
Step 3
Select "Properties" from the Menu
Scroll to the bottom of the context menu and click on Properties. A dialog box will open showing various details about the file.
4
🗂️
Step 4
Go to the "Details" Tab
Inside the Properties window, click on the Details tab at the top. This tab shows all the metadata stored within the file, including author, device, and revision information.
5
🔗
Step 5
Click on "Remove Properties and Personal Information"
At the bottom of the Details tab, you will see a link that reads "Remove Properties and Personal Information". Click on it to open the removal tool.
6
🛡️
Step 6
Select "Remove the Following Properties from This File"
A new dialog will appear. Choose the option labelled "Remove the following properties from this file" (not the copy option). This allows you to choose which properties to remove directly from the original file.
7
☑️
Step 7
Click "Select All" to Choose Every Property
Click the Select All button to tick every checkbox in the list. This ensures that all metadata — without exception — is marked for removal from the file.
8
✅
Step 8
Click "OK" to Apply
Click OK to confirm. The properties will be permanently removed from the file. Your file is now clean and ready to be uploaded as your assignment.
🎉

All Done — Your File is Ready to Upload!

You have successfully removed all personal metadata from your file. You may now proceed to upload your assignment through the designated submission portal. If you face any difficulties, please reach out to your coordinator.
